**Jarvis** – AI-Powered Voice Assistant  

Jarvis is your personal **AI** voice assistant built using Python.  
It can listen to your commands, open websites, play songs, read live news, and even chat intelligently using Google’s Gemini AI.  

---

**Features** 

**Voice Activation** – Say “Jarvis” to wake the assistant.  
**Web Automation** – Instantly open Google, YouTube, Facebook, or LinkedIn.  
**Music Control** – Play songs from your own music library file.  
**Live News Updates** – Hear the latest headlines using the News API.  
**AI Conversations** – Get smart and friendly replies powered by Gemini.  
**Speech Output** – Natural speech using Google Text-to-Speech (`gTTS`).  

---

**Technologies Used**  

**Python 3** 
**SpeechRecognition** – For capturing voice commands  
**gTTS** – For converting text to speech  
**Pygame** – For playing audio  
**Google Generative AI (Gemini API)** – For intelligent responses  
**Requests** – For fetching news articles  
**Webbrowser** – For opening websites  
**pyttsx3** – Backup speech engine
